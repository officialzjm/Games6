Supported Systems

NES runs on jsNES. GBA runs on EmulatorJS (mGBA core) — loaded on demand from CDN the first time you pick a GBA ROM.

Controls
NES

Keyboard: Arrows/WASD, Z/J/N (A), X/K/M (B), Enter/Space (Start), Shift/C (Select). On touch devices, use the on-screen pad.

GBA

EmulatorJS handles its own input — configure it from the in-emulator menu (gear icon).
