Supported Systems

Use NES for the most stable experience. GBA is available for testing and development-oriented previews.

Controls
NES

Arrows or WASD for movement, Z or J or N for A, X or K or M for B, Enter or Space for Start, Shift or C for Select.

GBA

Same base layout as NES, plus Q for L and E for R.
